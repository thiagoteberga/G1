Why choose Iglu?

iGLU is the integrated platform that will transform your business management experience into something more easy and intuitive. We offer simple solutions, agile and fully digital for your company increase productivity, accelerate results and take off in the business world.
Our platform has unified login services, case management, electronic document management, systems integration and data analysis, in addition to storing data in cloud structure and mobile access wherever you are. They are:

• SingleLog - Unified Access
You have confused your passwords and ended up blocking your access to one of the systems of your company? The systems that you use have different security levels so that you need multiple passwords? Don't worry. With the SingleLog you will need a login and password to have access to all the systems used in your company . SingleLog brings security and ease to you and ends with the access problems.

• GProc - Process Management
GProc will boost the productivity of your business, bringing more efficiency to its processes and better control of operations. The graphic modeling tool will give you an advanced vision process, facilitating the detection of faults and minimize rework. In addition, the GProc has a central tasks where its employees can have real-time control of the status of each activity and digital solutions that facilitate interaction between people from different areas.

• GDoc - Electronic Document Management
No more delay its operations with the use of physical documents, with the GDocs you scan your documents and gains time in their operations. Gdocs is the perfect tool for you to share and access documents from your company intelligently and at any time. Find your files easily using the search tool, and edit your documents together without fear because the GDocs allows versioning, tracking, resource permitting and history of your files in a secure repository. And all of this is stored in the cloud.

• Integra - Integrating systems
Keep the integration between the various systems of a company is a big challenge because of the variety of Technologies and languages in which they are developed. Integrates allows these softwares work together, making your company management simpler, and give a more complete view about the way the applications are integrated.

• IndTool - Indicators and Dashboard
IndTool is a data analysis tool that uses information about what happens inside the platform Igloo to generate data that will facilitate the management of your business. With Indtool your company has a powerful BI tool to create views and dashboards, that analyze and also reveal trends. You can even share data with employees, encouraging them to use intelligent productivity information for better decision-making.
